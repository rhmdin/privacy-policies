Tiempo Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Tiempo is a presentation coaching companion for Keynote, developed by Rahmadina.

Information We Access

  • Microphone audio — used locally on your device to calculate your speaking pace (words per minute) during a live presentation session. Audio is processed in real-time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.
  • Presentation file name and slide/page information — used to detect slide transitions and associate pacing data with the correct presentation. Tiempo does not access or read the content of your slides.

Data Storage

All session data (recap history, pacing statistics) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Tiempo does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

Data Sharing

Tiempo does not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at rhmdin@icloud.com.
